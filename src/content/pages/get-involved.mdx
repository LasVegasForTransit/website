The 2027 legislative session is the moment. Whether you have five minutes, five hours, or a multi-year commitment to give, there is work that needs you.

## In five minutes

- **Subscribe to our newsletter** for legislative updates, project briefs, and ways to show up. Form below.
- **Follow us** on Instagram, Bluesky, or wherever you spend your time. Quote and share.
- **Tell one person** about the 2028 service cuts. Most Las Vegans don't know yet.

## In five hours

- **Come to an event.** Showing up at a meeting changes what's politically possible.
- **Write your legislator** about RTC funding before the 2027 session. We'll publish a template.
- **Document your bus stop.** Take a photo. Note the shade, the bench, the route board, the trash situation. We'll aggregate.

## In five years

- **Become a member.** A formal membership program is in the works for 2027 (see our membership strategy).
- **Lead a project.** Project leads run our outreach work — at a college, a high school, a faith community, a labor coalition. We'll train and back you up.
- **Run for something.** The most durable transit wins start with transit advocates becoming the people who hold the votes.

## If you have money

[Donate.](https://givebutter.com/lvbt) Every dollar buys signs, prints reports, pays for a year of website hosting, fuels a coalition meeting. We are entirely volunteer-run for now; donations make the difference between sending a letter to one legislator and sending one to twenty.

## If you run an organization

We are actively building a coalition. If you represent a labor union, a faith community, a business, a school, a healthcare system, or another nonprofit and want to talk about partnership, email **partners@lasvegasfortransit.org**.
