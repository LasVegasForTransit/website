---
title: About LVBT
summary: We are the grassroots advocacy organization fighting for world-class public transit and supportive land use in the Las Vegas Valley.
---

LVBT was founded on April 17, 2026 in response to a simple, urgent fact: the Regional Transportation Commission of Southern Nevada had warned the State Legislature that without dedicated funding, **42% of the Valley's bus service would be cut by 2028**. Las Vegas — a metropolitan area of 2.3 million people — was about to lose the largest bus network in the American West, with no organized public voice demanding the state act.

The Valley deserves better than that. So we got to work.

We are intentionally an organization that didn't exist until it had to. Cities that win transit fights — Phoenix, Salt Lake City, Albuquerque, Nashville, Austin — all share one ingredient: an organized constituency that legislators and agencies have to answer to. Las Vegas has not had that. We're building it.
