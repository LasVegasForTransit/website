Read

LM3 is the strongest of the four — the bulb at the chevron apex is the one earned ornament that quietly says "this references the Welcome sign" without any literal sign element on the page. LM2 is the right call if you want zero ornament and pure structural geometry. LM4 probably reads more "antenna" than "sign" once you scale it down. LM1 is too austere — it's just a divider line; the structural-bracket idea disappears.
