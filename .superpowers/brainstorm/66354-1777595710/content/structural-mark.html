Recommendation

BM2 is probably the right register: subtle enough that the casual viewer just sees clean architecture, recognizable enough that any Las Vegan reads "Welcome sign" instantly. BM1 is the most disciplined option if you want to err on the side of restraint. BM4 probably crosses the line — at that point you're rendering the sign, not referencing it.
