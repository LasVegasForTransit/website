What changed
  • Mark and wordmark are now one SVG file with one coordinate space — they scale and align as one object.
  • Chevron arm endpoints sit exactly on cap-top and baseline (y=1 and y=41 in the 42-tall viewBox). No overshoot.
  • Stroke uses butt linecap on the lines so they terminate precisely instead of extending past with rounded ends. The chevron apex still uses linejoin=round for a clean corner.
  • Gap between chevron tip and "L" reduced from 14px → 6px (in B) or 4px (in C). The lockup now reads as one composition.
  • Construction is documented in the alignment-guides view — every coordinate is intentional.
