Level 3 · Translated

Sign grammar, system voice

Pure Public Sans + ink. The sign-derived inflection: a single column of six 3px ink dots as a margin marker — the bulb idea, abstracted to data-grid punctuation, used once. The label gets a tiny diamond glyph (4px). Earned ornament, sub-pixel craft, one voice.

Silicon Valley
The shift

Level 1 uses the sign's decoration as a frame. Level 2 abandons the sign entirely. Level 3 uses the sign's grammar — the rhythm of small bulb-dots as punctuation, the diamond as a 4px label-marker, the typographic hierarchy of micro-label / huge display / small caption. Same brand DNA, no costume.

If Level 3 is the right framework, the next step is rewriting all the primitives I proposed earlier through this lens — and saving everything literal-sign for one or two genuine hero moments per project. Tell me to proceed and I'll consolidate.
