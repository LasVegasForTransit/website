What changed from the rejected version
  • Stroke went from 5px saturated yellow to 1.25px ink hairline.
  • Bulb count went from 28 yellow to 12 ink + 4 cardinal accents. Less is more: the eye reads the rhythm without being shouted at.
  • Eliminated the yellow-orange color clash entirely on the paper version.
  • The night version uses bulbs as light sources (gradient halos, bright cores) rather than flat dots — atmospheric craft instead of flat decoration.
  • Generous interior padding; type breathes; clear hierarchy (small label → huge number → small caption).

If this construction quality reads right, the design framework holds and we can lock the direction. Bulb letters become a brand signature; the diamond stat is reserved for a few key statistics; everything else is the existing Public Sans + ink/paper system, with one or two literal-sign hero moments per project.
